About Me

Sobre Mi

I'm currently based in the beautiful city of Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I am passionate about aesthetics because I believe that an accessible and clean design can make a product unique.

I have worked on many graphic projects, such as logos, album covers, illustrations and brand design. My knowledge includes, but is not limited to, the Adobe package such as Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects and Premiere.

2 years ago I started to be interested in web development and design, and since then I have been doing courses and studying non-stop. I have extensive knowledge in HTML and CSS and to date I continue to perfect myself in JavaScript.

I am also passionate about languages. My native language is Spanish, although I have a bilingual level in English since I practiced it all my life. Lately I've also started to practice Italian.
